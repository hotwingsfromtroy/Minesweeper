× How To Play

Select a difficulty level to start.
Click on a tile to reveal it.
Alt+Click on a tile to flag it. Flagged tiles cannot be revealed.
Alt+Click on a flagged tile to unflag it.
Each mine-free tile is either blank or displays a number.
This number corresponds to the number of mines in that cell's vicinity. It ranges from 1 to 8.
Use this information to identify mines and avoid them.
Good Luck!
Try not to die too many times.
